Build a complete SaaS Multi-Tenant POS (Point of Sale) system with modern UI/UX and scalable architecture.

PROJECT NAME:
Universal POS SaaS

OVERVIEW:
Create a cloud-based POS platform where multiple businesses can register, subscribe, and use the system under their own brand.

The system must support multiple business types:

1. Food & Beverage (Restaurant, Cafe, Coffee Shop)
2. Fashion & Retail Store
3. Salon & Beauty
4. Minimarket & Grocery Store

The application must be Multi-Tenant, meaning each business only sees its own data while the developer/admin can manage all tenants from a Super Admin Dashboard.

==================================================
MULTI TENANT SYSTEM
==================================================

Each business registration creates a unique tenant.

Database tables must include:

tenant_id

for data isolation.

Examples:

tenants
users
products
categories
orders
customers
expenses
employees
subscriptions

All data queries must automatically filter by tenant_id.

No tenant can access another tenant's data.
